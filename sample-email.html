Congratulations, and thank you!


Dear CPE Graduate,

It was inspiring to see you cross that stage and receive your professional certificate from our college! We're proud of your hard work and can't wait to hear what you'll do next. Thanks for letting CPE be part of your educational journey.

Meanwhile, here are some things to look forward to:
  • Pics: Browse photos from graduation on Facebook. Tag yourself and bask in the praise of your friends. You deserve it.
  • Resume update: Did you take a headshot? Download your photo and use it on LinkedIn where you can spruce up your profile to include CPE under your Education.
  • Discount: If you need more professional development later, our grads get a 10% off CPE certificates.
  • Paying it forward: Leave words of wisdom for others who come behind you with a review on Google or Facebook.
  • Career growth: Looking for a job? Our graduates have access to Handshake, an all-in-one career management system.
Congrats again, and stay in touch!

Feel free to contact us anytime at KSUprofessional@kennesaw.edu or just reply to this message. We get a lot of emails, but messages from our grads are some of our favorites.
